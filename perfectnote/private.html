Private Lessons

Class Info:

Private Lessons are offered for students who would like a one on one atmosphere. The Instructor will focus mainly on what the student wants to learn, and will be able to help them develop their playing more fully. During a Private Lesson, the Instructor will be able to adapt to the students learning speed. Also, our ways of teaching will be centered around the students progression, building on their strengths, and working on their weaknesses.

Private Lessons are centered around the student, therefore we are willing to work with other dates or times that will be easier for the student. However, if at all possible, we would like to keep to our preferred dates which are set in the Class Schedules.

Pricing:

Perfect Note Instructors can teach Private Lessons either in our regular classroom, or if the student requests, we can teach them in a more convenient area (Home, School, etc.). We understand that some students will not be able to get to our classroom, for whatever reason, thus we are willing to drive to them. For our Instructor to drive to your specific location, we will add $5 to the Lesson Price.

  • Specific Location: $50
  • Classroom: $45
