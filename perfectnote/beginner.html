Beginner Lessons

Is this for me?

Our Beginner Lessons are offered for students who have never picked up a Guitar, or for students who are just trying to get down the basics. If you know a few Chords, or just trying to get down your strumming, these lessons are for you. For instance, if you only know around four or five chords, and struggle with getting the strumming down, these lessons are perfect for you. Our Instructors are willing to help students at all levels of playing to understand the basics of playing the Guitar.

What if I'm not sure about beginner lessons?

If you have been signed up for beginner lessons and feel that you aren't progressing, we can easily move you to the Intermediate course. Also, if the Instructor feels that your skill is above the Beginner Level, you will have the option to jump to the Intermediate Lessons.
