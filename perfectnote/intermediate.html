Intermediate Lessons

Is this for me?

Intermediate Lessons are designed for students who have played the guitar, are used to strumming and understand basic chord progression. For instance, if you know around six or seven songs, and feel comfortable with your overall playing , these lessons are for you. Perfect Note has created these lessons for people who want to continue their progression and learn different styles of playing.

I want to excel!

This class is also designed for students who are at a stand still in their current skills, or are struggling with certain strumming patterns, picking patterns, or just overall songs.
