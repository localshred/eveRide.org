how does it all work?

The message board works a lot like a chat room, except without a streaming conversation. It's more like leaving a note on the counter for your mom to read, but anyone else in the house can read it too. People can reply to the subject you start with your post, or can create a new subject like you did. We currently only have one category (eveRide), and two forums (everpark, and humanitarian events) to which you can post, but the forums are located under the category (like a directory tree), so pretty much don't worry about the Category. So what does all that mean? Select either everpark, or humanitarian events to post a comment to. It's not as complicated as it seems, so play around with it, and Tell a Friend to go and sign up too.

do I have to register?

NO, you may post a comment anonymously. But if you want us to contact you with questions and maybe hook you up with some stuff, registering is quick, easy, and FREE way to do it.

For any other questions or concerns or whatever else, email me at webmaster@everide.org, and I'll try and get back to you as soon as possible. Thanks, and enjoy the message board!

how do I post a comment?

Once you get to the Forum, click on the Register link located center top of the main forum page. From there you need to input just the regular information, and once you're signed up, you can post as many comments as you want.
