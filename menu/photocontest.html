We'd love to show off your pictures! Get exposure and just show people how sick you really are.

Here's the cut of the jib. Scan your picture and send it to us. Winning photos will be displayed right in the middle of eveRide's home page along with a caption. Here are some tips to score bonus points with the judges:

  • Pictures don't necessarily have to be of snow sports. It's encouraged, but submit whatever your favorite pictures may be.
  • Please keep it clean. Send us stuff that your mother would approve of.
  • Bonus points are given for creative outfits while riding. (Like hot-pink body suits.)
  • Make a caption telling us what you're doing and why you are so awesome, or we'll make one for you.
  • Please don't doctor your photos, keep it real.
  • Don't forget to credit the photographer!
  • Say "cheese!"
