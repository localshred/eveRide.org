The Sponsor Solution

what are we thinking?
When I originally thought of how I could fund the eveRide cause I was considering getting funds from venture capitalists, banks, and selling out to ski-resorts. But who would that help? The entire time I would be held back by the decisions of others. The VCs would tell me how to run things to make them more money. Nobody has fun with banks, you've probably dealt with them before. And the ski-resort idea? That would kill the whole root of why I wanted to make this thing in the first place.

eveRide will never be about money. I figured eveRide could support some awesome humanitarian programs and reward supporting businesses while still making the everparks. I had no idea who or why anybody would donate enough to fund the parks though, especially when there was really nothing in it for them but a pat on the back and maybe a free t-shirt.

Thank heaven for PBS. While channel surfing I crossed one of those rad nature shows they put on there about ants, and at the end it showed some commercials for their "proud sponsors." They also thanked all the viewers like me. Why couldn't eveRide be like that? Well, it can!

Benefits of Sponsorship

what's in it for your business?
If your business has been thinking about sponsoring eveRide, you're on the right track. The advantages that follow sponsorship will get you thinking. We want to give back to our sponsors more than what they give us, and we're prepared to do that in a variety of ways.
respect earns respect
By sponsoring eveRide you show your respect for the your customers and the entire snowriding community. You show this by letting them know that your company is willing to lay down some funds so they can ride at a better park for less. That's something you really can be proud of, and the community and your customers will respect your organization for it.
be heard directly by your target market
You know on PBS they show a short commercial provided by their sponsor? You'll get the same. With the revolutionary terrain and ideas behind the everparks, there are sure to be plenty of people hitting those slopes. If you haven't seen what the everparks are all about we strongly recommend you to check it out. We want our sponsors to be heard by these masses! Here are some things we'll do for our sponsors:
  1. Put you on the pass! We are planning to make a membership card for the everparks. We'll put our top sponsors on them! Riders will be able to get them in only a few places: online and at sponsoring businesses. Get the target market into your store by carrying these passes! Have them show their pass to get discounts on your goods. Our sponsors will have access to personal information gathered with the passes, and will be able to advertize direct through email, mailings, and heaven forbid the phone. If you want to know more about the EverPass feel free to contact us.
  2. Demo-stands on demand. Although magazine ads, banners, TV commercials, radio spots, and internet banners are all effective ways to spread the word about your product, sometimes buyers need to feel, touch, smell, taste, try, and ride what you're selling. They can get to know the quality of your product first hand when they demo or sample it. We'll give you access to on-hill product kiosks where riders can 'test drive' what you're selling.
  3. Name rails, jumps, and other terrain after your business. Say we put your banner on a rail. From then on, that rail is naturally named after your company by the riders.
  4. Display an array of banners in places where they will be recognized. We'll hang them overhead, put them on our fences, gates, lifts, buildings, equipment, our white glove groomers and anywhere else you might want them.
  5. Web site banner space. If your organization has a website you want your market to see and would like a banner on the eveRide homepage, it's done!
the shockwave
This is where your sponsorship will be rewarded. At the everparks there will not only be a slew of snowriders, but film crews, magazine photographers, competition spectators, competition TV coverage, and concert crowds. All of these folks will be displaying your banners in the movies they film, pictures they take, stories they write, competitions they cover, and the concerts they play. Count on it, your banners will be seen far and wide.
breaks for sponsors and donors
Donating to eveRide is considered a charitable contribution, which means you pay less to the IRS.
who will hear your message?
Are the people listening? Will they hear? Do they care? Find out by looking at our stoked signers list!

How to Sponsor eveRide

how to sponsor
If you are a small company trying to boost recognition with the public, or a big company wanting to score points with your target market, it's always a good idea to be down with the righteous cause. Show them your support for eveRide and the programs it promotes. We're not going to have you fill out a form or anything, but we'd like to talk to you in person. We'll get things started with an email. If you're interested in sponsorship and would like to know more, we'll answer all your questions.

Ad-Swap

you help us, we help you!
Just like you need to spread the information on your awesome business, we need to spread the word about eveRide.org and the everparks! During this introductory phase, if you'd like to do a link-swap for a banner on our page, or an ad-swap, just email us and we'll get it all set up!

Just email us!

Donation Destinations

donation destinations
We thought we'd put this in just to make sure you knew and we remembered our promise to the community, to our sponsors, and to ourselves. 100% of all money donated to eveRide through individuals, sponsors, or whoever will go directly back into the eveRide cause to build the everparks, help the community, and to support the humanitarian projects we'll have. That means none of those funds will be touched by the founders for personal gain now or in the future.
