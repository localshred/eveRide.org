We want this to be a park for the people, by the people... so naturally we want the people to design our terrain! Let your imagination go wild with this! Draw up or describe whatever you think would be fun to ride, scan it, and send it to us. Boxes, rails, pipe features, jumps, tubing runs, whatever! We're not kidding when we say we want the most revolutionary terrain at the everparks, and we want your help to make it that way! There is nothing too wild!
